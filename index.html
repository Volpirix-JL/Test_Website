What is the TVA?

The Tennessee Valley Authority is a federally owned corporation created in 1933 to modernize the Tennessee Valley through electricity generation, flood control, and economic development.

Mission

TVA’s mission is to provide affordable electricity, manage natural resources, and support economic growth across seven states in the southeastern United States.

TVA Today

Today, the TVA remains the nation’s largest public power provider, supplying energy to millions of homes and businesses while continuing environmental stewardship.
